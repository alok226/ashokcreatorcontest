Q1: Reels ki limit kitni hai?

Agar aap 3 reels banate hain, toh un teeno ke combined views aur combined engagements plus kiye jayenge. Single video wale ka sirf 1 video hi count hoga.

Sirf official **@ashokthefamilyshop** account ke saath collab zaroori hai. Friends ke saath collab accept nahi kiya jayega rules ke mutabik.
