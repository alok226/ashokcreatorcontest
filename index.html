Q1: Reels ki limit kitni hai?

Kam se kam 1 reel banana mandatory hai. Jeetne ke chances badhane ke liye aap maximum 3 reels bana sakte hain, unka score combine karke bonus milega.

Q2: 3 Reels ka calculation kaise hoga?

Agar aap 3 reels banate hain, toh un teeno ke **combined views** aur teeno ke **combined engagements** plus kiye jayenge. 1 video wale ka sirf single video hi count hoga.

Q3: Collab ka kya rule hai?

Sirf official **@ashokthefamilyshop** account ke saath collab zaroori hai. Friends ya doosre pages ke saath collab allow nahi hai rules ke mutabik.
